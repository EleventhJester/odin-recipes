Strogonoff


Strogonoff de Brasileiro finished

Brazilian Chicken Stroganoff, also known as Strogonoff de Frango, is a creamy and savory dish that combines juicy chicken with a rich tomato and cream-based sauce, often served with white rice and shoestring potatoes. This dish has a unique Brazilian twist.

Ingedrients

  • Chicken: 1 lb boneless chicken breasts (cut into 1 inch cubes)
  • Red Bell Pepper: 1 (chopped and diced)
  • Onion: 2/3 (chopped and diced)
  • Garlic: 4 cloves (minced)
  • Olive Oil: 3 tsp
  • Tomato Sauce: 15 oz
  • Water: 1/2 cup
  • Heavy Cream: 1 cup
  • Red Wine Vinegar: 2 tbsp
  • Granulated Garlic: 3 tsp (or to taste)
  • Ground Black Pepper: 1/2 tsp (or to taste)
  • Salt: 3 tsp (or to taste)
  • Parsley: (for garnishing)
  • Shoestring Potato Sticks: (for garnishing)
  1. Cut the chicken breasts into 1-inch cubes and season with red wine vinegar, salt, ground black pepper, and granulated garlic. Set aside.
  2. Chop and dice the red pepper and onion. Mince the garlic cloves.
  3. Once the oil is heated, add the red pepper and onion. Saute until the onions are translucent..
  4. Add the minced garlic cloves and saute for 20-25 seconds, lowering the heat if needed to avoid burning the garlic..
  5. Immediately add the chicken and mushrooms and cook until the chicken is completely white, stirring frequently, for about 5-7 minutes..
  6. Add the tomato sauce and water, bring to a boil, and add salt to taste. Reduce the heat to low and simmer, stirring as needed..
  7. Once the chicken is cooked through, add the heavy cream and continue simmering on low for another 5-7 minutes until the sauce has thickened.
  8. Finally, top with shoestring potato sticks and serve over Brazilian Rice for the ultimate Brazilian feast.
