Fresh Salmon with Bell Peppers

This light and healthy salmon recipe features oven-roasted salmon fillets paired with sweet bell peppers, garlic, and a drizzle of olive oil for a simple but flavorful meal.

Ingredients

  • Salmon Fillets: 2 (about 6 oz each)
  • Red Bell Pepper: 1 (sliced)
  • Yellow Bell Pepper: 1 (sliced)
  • Garlic: 2 cloves (minced)
  • Olive Oil: 2 tbsp
  • Lemon Juice: 1 tbsp
  • Salt: 1 tsp (or to taste)
  • Black Pepper: 1/2 tsp (or to taste)
  • Fresh Parsley: for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place the salmon fillets on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. Arrange the sliced red and yellow bell peppers around the salmon.
  4. Drizzle the olive oil and lemon juice over the salmon and peppers.
  5. Sprinkle minced garlic, salt, and black pepper evenly on top.
  6. Bake for 15-18 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.
  7. Remove from the oven and garnish with fresh parsley before serving.
